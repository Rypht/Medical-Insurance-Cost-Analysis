Medical Insurance Cost Analysis Dashboard

🎯 Objective

To analyse how demographic and health factors impact insurance charges and identify high-risk individuals.

Dataset includes:
1,338 records
Features include: age, BMI, smoking status, children, region


*Key Insights

• Smoking increases insurance costs by ~3–4x

• Costs rise steadily with age

• High BMI significantly increases costs, especially for smokers


*Business Recommendations:

• Use risk-based pricing
  Smokers cost ~3–4x more → charge higher premiums

• Promote prevention
  Offer discounts for quitting smoking and improving BMI

• Target high-risk groups
  Focus on: older + smoker + high BMI → highest costs

• Segment customers
  Create pricing tiers based on age, BMI, and smoking
